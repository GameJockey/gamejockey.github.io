Haven’t written in ages. No, I’m not out of the game. I’m back in my shitty, little hometown. It’s misery. But nonetheless, for the past two months, I’ve been going out literally every single fucking day. I’m fucking hungry. I want this shit.

Anyways, there are a few FRs I’ll share these days from this year that were worth writing. For tonight I have something fresh that happened like three hours ago. Fuck, dude, the game will be the end of me…

Thank god I didn’t stay at home tonight instead, as I sort of wanted to work and didn’t expect much happening in the field. Little did I know…

August 8th, 2016 Monday (Written right after coming home)

Field Report: A Night To Remember

“I’d rather regret doing something, than regret not doing it” —RSD Todd

A Different Kind of Virginity

It’s just us now.

I haven’t felt this awkward in weeks.

I always thought of myself kind of beneath her. She and her friends were too cool for me, so we’ve never really hung out; I’ve never had the balls to act normally around her.

She was so much better than me, so perfect in her loving, you-only-live-once, adventurous personality, unlike me, the little conservative, logical, chode boy. I’ve always found ways to be that little fag in front of her.

I look at her amazing hips and ass, and wonder do I go home and cry myself to sleep, or awkwardly walk around pretending to type on my phone.

She’s sits down and starts rolling a joint. I’m awkwardly sitting on my bike, looking around, occasionally glancing at her. I awkwardly ask something, hoping she responds well. She does.

Slowly, I keep asking more questions about what she’s doing with her life and shit, and start to joke around. I spark up and we have a somewhat fun conversation.

I feel relieved—she actually talks to me and doesn’t think of me as a piece of shit who’s so beneath her that I’m not even worth her breath. This mental image I’ve had of her is complete and utter bullshit. This unreachable, bitchy-towards-chodes amazing girl is just a silly, little girl, a silly, little, imperfect kid who just wants to have fun, not entertain value-leeching chodes.

As I’m slowly unstifling, my bro arrives. At this point, I’m at the cause of this whole interaction. I’m the center of attention, I’m the shit, and I know it.

“Hey… Come on,” she says.

I look her straight in the eye, marveling at her beauty, and think for a few seconds.

Fuck it, dude. If I was gonna do this with anyone ever in my life—I’d choose her, and her only. She’s the only girl that lives for those beautiful-but-brief moments, the only one that really knows how to be truly alive and fucking enjoy it fully.

She gives it to me with her sweet, sweet hands—and I take a puff. And a few more.

“This could be Heaven or this could be Hell…”

Embracing The Unknown

“Just relax,” she says laughingly.

I’m shivering—it feels like it’s minus twenty fucking degrees. I want her in my arms more than anything I’ve ever wanted in my fucking life. But I’m too much of a faggot to just hug her. I see in her eyes that she’s fucking begging me to do it on the inside.

She plays Hotel California by Eagles on her phone and keeps on giggling. We hum along.

“If your hoodie wasn’t too small for me, I’d ask you for it,” I say. She laughs hysterically.

It’s one of those moments right now. Those beautiful moments between two lovers, little moments of us looking into each other’s eyes, moments worth dying for. Moments that I’ve been chasing my whole fucking life. Here’s one: fucking take it.

Here we are, night, sitting next to each other, listening to beautiful, lovey-dovey music, both high, giggling like little happy kids, fucking living—and then I let us both down. I wanna cry right now, but I can’t even do that.

I can just tell she wants it so much. Oh, if she knew how much I want it too. I just smile and try to enjoy the moment, while simultaneously hating myself for not going for it.

We have fun. I’m still shaking like a motherfucker from being cold.

She plays Rehab by Amy Winehouse and teases me that I need a rehab too.

I’m like “I should’ve said ‚no, no, no’” and she’s like ‚“Yes, yes, yes” while laughing at me. I realize that even now in this amazing moment, I’m being a logical, conservative little fag who can’t enjoy the fucking moment by saying I regret doing it. I fucking don’t, I’m just a goddamn pussy.

At one point she says how she loves this joy of being high, just giggling and enjoying it all. I feel like I fucking ruined it for her by being dumb.

I keep thinking about ways to escalate but I just fucking can’t. The best “excuse” I’m coming up with is that I’m cold and that I need her to warm me up. Occasionally, she would touch my shoulder as she’s talking but I never really knew how to touch her, and not have it be awkward.

“C’mon, will these guys leave already, ” I say, referring to her little brother and some dudes we know, who are hanging out nearby, likely even listening to our conversation, knowing them and their jealousy of me suddenly hanging out with her.

They actually start leaving right then. She sees her little brother leaving with them, jumps up, mumbles something totally nonsensical like, “I can’t get home after my brother, see ya,” and literally storms off without a giving a single fuck.

I’m just sitting there by myself, staring at her, at them all leaving me by myself like I’m not even here. What in the actual fuck just happened?!

I feel confused, and I feel very used. She enjoyed my company and then just ditched me in a heartbeat.

I ride my bike, roaming the town for about half an hour just processing this, trying not to think of what the hell that was.

Fuck my life…

Conclusion

So, what was that? There are a few possible explanations to this, and the one that makes the most sense to me right now is that she doesn’t want those guys to think that “oh there she goes fucking yet another dude.” That’s the most likely reason why.

Another one is that she actually doesn’t want to be totally one-on-one with me for whatever reason. We had a good time though, so I don’t see why would she hesitate. Maybe she’s just fucking pissed that I was a chode, I would be. I don’t know.

So, checking Facebook after coming home, she most likely went out to a nearby town to a bar with her friends right after ditching me, to drink and party with her crew. I know I shouldn’t be obsessing over it, and I’m certainly not, but yeah, I feel used by her.

I know, I am a chode right now. Fuck this shit, I’m better than that.

My confidence used to be fucking divine, I mean, I was the coolest motherfucker ever, I was a true fucking unshakable god of all and there was fucking nothing that could make me question myself, but in the last few months, being without a proper purpose and path in life, I feel fucking lost and my confidence has waned. I need to cut this fucking faggotry out and be a goddamn god that I am.

Anyways, I need to work on this shit. Fuck it. I’m a mess right now. I’m cutting into my sleep to write this, just because “I love her so much and fuck everything else, she’s worth it all,” which is exactly why I’m a chode these days. Purpose over women is what drives true confidence. I sorta lack that shit these days.

There’s far too much emotional faggotry in this report, but what can I do, being a man is hard when you’re a chode.

I’ve spent far too much time writing this shit so I’m gonna stop myself right here and head to bed. I’m don’t even wanna sleep, dreaming about shit will just poke that wound even more. Fuck it.

Wish me some goddamn luck. Cheers.
